## Scenario
A fintech firm processes online payments across the UK and EU. The organization seeks ISO 27001 alignment to strengthen governance oversight and regulatory compliance.

---

## Objectives

- Define ISMS boundaries.
- Identify internal and external interested parties.
- Establish business and regulatory drivers.

---

## Scope Statement Example

The ISMS applies to:

- Payment processing platforms
- Customer data hosting environments
- Corporate IT systems supporting UK and EU operations

Excluded:

- HR-only SaaS tools with no sensitive data

---

## Interested Parties

| Party | Requirements |
|------|----------------|
| Customers | Data protection and uptime |
| Regulators | PCI DSS, GDPR compliance |
| Board | Risk transparency |
| Vendors | Clear security requirements |

---

## Governance Alignment

- ISMS reporting line to Security Steering Committee.
- Risk posture communicated quarterly to Board.
