# Lab 02 – Risk Assessment & Treatment

## Scenario
The fintech company identifies multiple exposure points during cloud migration.

---

## Risk Methodology

- Threat × Vulnerability × Impact scoring
- CIA-based impact analysis
- Qualitative severity levels

---

## Sample Risk Register

| Asset | Risk | Likelihood | Impact | Treatment |
|------|-------|--------------|---------|-------------|
| Payment Gateway | DDoS outage | Medium | High | Transfer + Mitigate |
| CRM Platform | PII exposure | Low | High | Mitigate |
| Admin portals | Credential theft | High | High | Remove |

---

## Risk Treatment Approach

Accepted risks remain **within tolerance boundaries defined by governance policy** and approved by senior leadership.
